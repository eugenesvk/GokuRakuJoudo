  |New label      	| Side 	| Must/opt 	| Key    	|Old	|

__NB!__

  - Left/Right side indicators __must__ be at their respective sides (‹Left, Right›)
  - Optional﹖ indicator __must__ bet at the Right side and __after__ the side indicator (✓`⎈›﹖` ✗`⎈﹖›`)
